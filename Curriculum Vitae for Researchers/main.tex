% About Me
I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Joshua Vogelstein, Dr. Pratik Chaudhari (UPenn), and Dr. Carey E. Priebe (JHU). My work primarily focuses on building deep learning and statistical machine learning methods for out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization and continually learning systems. Additionally, I have a keen interest in the applications of computer vision, geometric deep learning, signal processing, and biomedical data science.
